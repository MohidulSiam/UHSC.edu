UTTARA HigH ScHOoL AND COLLEGE

Uttara High School and College is one of the oldest schools in Dhaka. This school has reputation for good results in the SSC and HSC exams. The environment of the school is very helpful for the students to study and the teachers here are also highly trained. it was thought as a wonderful idea to have a public School of a very high standard which will admit boys and girls from all walks of life strictly on merit through admission tests. It is expected that their academic training at this great institution would make them physically and mentally sound enough to adjust and complete with the rest in the pursuit of higher studies. They would eventually turn out as complete men and women having full confidence in their ability in the challenging world.

Actually UHSC is the First school at Uttara under MPO. Established in 1985 and started college from 2009. Almost 9000 students are studying here under 200 qualified teacher. A big playground and a nice cafeteria are it's main attraction. This institute is maintained by a Governing Body committee contain 15 wise member. Nick name UHSC.
